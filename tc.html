    • General Terms & Conditions of Entry to Bala cinimas

    Customers who are in possession of a valid ticket will only be permitted to enter the designated cinema for the specified duration of the show printed on the ticket.

    Customers who are disruptive, unwell, antisocial or violent will be asked to leave the premises immediately without any entitlement to a refund and Bala cinimas Management reserves the right to determine what constitutes disruptive, unwell, antisocial or violent behavior.

    Mobile phones, cameras and all other electronic devices must be switched off or on silent mode before you enter any cinema.

    Photography and/or videography is/are strictly prohibited inside any cinema. You are not permitted to bring mobile phones, cameras and/or recording equipment of any kind into any cinema for the purposes of illegal activity. We reserve the right to carry out random bag checks and refuse equipment at the sole discretion of the Bala cinimas Management.

    Smoking (including any form of electronic cigarette or other smoking devices) is not permitted anywhere in any part of the cinema or surrounding lobbies and public areas of the Bala cinimas premises.

    You must keep your personal belongings with you at all times. We accept no responsibility for the loss, theft, alteration or damage to any personal items brought into the premises.

    You may not bring any dangerous and/or illegal items into any part of the cinema or surrounding lobbies and public areas of the Bala cinimas premises at any time.

    Keep prams and strollers out of the way. While they can be taken into the cinema, please don’t leave them in the aisles or exits. They may be positioned in designated wheelchair areas, however please note wheelchair users have priority, so staff may direct prams or strollers to be positioned elsewhere.
